How Eukaryotes came to be

  • Endosymbiotic theory
    • Symbiosis - When two living organisms work together
    • Endo - inner
  • The membrane of the nucleus and other membrane-bound organelles most likely came from deep folds in the cell membrane.
  • Smaller oxygen-living bacteria were consumed by a bigger cell, but somehow survived, so they became Mitochondria.
    • This theory was invented because the Mitochondria has it's own DNA, which is different than the main cell's DNA.
  • In plant cells, the same thing happened but with photosynthesizing bacteria.
  • This is just a theory, though.
